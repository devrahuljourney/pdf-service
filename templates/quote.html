Day 1: Welcome to Amsterdam
Arrive in Amsterdam, settle into your hotel, and relax before tomorrow's scenic evening cruise before sharing a welcome dinner with your fellow travellers.
Day 2: Tulips & Timeless Crafts
Spend the day at Keukenhof's blooming gardens, explore the heritage of Zaanse Schans, and enjoy a relaxed afternoon.
Day 3: Capitals in Transit
Make the journey from Paris via Brussels, strolling through Brussels' Grand Place before arriving in the City of Light.
Day 4: Versailles, Paris Icons and a Seine Cruise
Step into the splendour of Versailles, discover Paris' most iconic spots, ascend the Eiffel Tower, and conclude the day with a Seine River dinner cruise.
Day 5: A Free Day in Paris or Disneyland
Shape the day to your interests; wander through Paris, boutiques, and neighbourhoods, or have an optional magical experience at Disneyland Paris.
Day 6: Burgundy Countryside and Arrival in Switzerland
Pause in Dijon and continue across scenic routes to reach the natural beauty of Switzerland.
Day 7: GoldenPass Scenic Rail, Interlaken & Optional Jungfraujoch Experience
Journey through Switzerland's alpine heart on the GoldenPass Line, with an optional chance to choose between a relaxed lakeside afternoon or a breathtaking ascent to Jungfraujoch – Top of Europe!
Day 8: Mt. Titlis and Lucerne
Rise to the snowy heights of Mt. Titlis, enjoy an alpine lunch, explore Lucerne's Chapel Bridge, Lion's treat, and unwind amid the beauty of Lucerne on a cruise with appetisers.
Day 9: Rhine Falls, Zurich and Lindt Chocolate Factory
Feel the rush of the Rhine Falls on a boat ride, tour the cultural centre of Zurich, and savour sweet moments inside the Lindt Chocolate Factory.
Day 10: Switzerland to Italy: Lakeside Scenery & Milan Highlights
Cross into Italy, passing through the scenic Lugano lake region and past Lake Como, arriving in Milan for an orientation tour of this iconic landmarks, before continuing to Padova for dinner and an overnight stay.
Day 11: Canals, Colours & Venetian Craft
Experience Venice by boat and gondola, then explore the shining glass workshops of Murano and the pastel tones of Burano before continuing through Tuscany.
Day 12: Pisa, Tuscany & Medieval Dreams
Capture the Leaning Tower of Pisa, stroll the medieval streets of San Gimignano, savour a gelato, and arrive in Rome by evening.
Day 13: Witness the Eternal City
Trace the capital's ancient heart and the Vatican's treasures, from the Colosseum and Sistine Chapel to the Fountain to St. Peter's Basilica.
Day 14: Farewell to Europe from Rome
Depart the Eternal City carrying home the memories, moments, and stories gathered along the way.
